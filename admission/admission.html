Nakuru girls high school was officially declared a standalone national school in the year 2006 after being separated from the Nakuru School then.After 2006,the school began its own admission process only admitting girls to the institution being a girls boarding school.Over the years, it has recorded a great improvement in the rates of admission improving year in year out due to the good performance it always poses during the national examinations results. The Nakuru Girls' High School has proven to be one of the most preferred high schools for the students.

The graph shows the trends in admission rates of our school over the past fifteen years.Each year we continue to enroll plenty of students due to the high perfomance we always register in the national exams.Due to this ,alot of the girls select this school as their school of choice and thus translating and explaining the rising trend in the rates of admission at the Nakuru Girls High School.This graph explicitly shows that this school is everyones choice.
